How to Play

The traditional Prisoner's Dilemma scenario sees two prisoners placed into individual interrogation rooms, and they must choose to cooperate with or defect from one another. This will determine their individual prison sentences. Both prisoner's have been given the same deal, that being if they defect from their partner and rat them out they will receive a reduced sentence. The prisoner's may also remain silent and cooperate with one another to receive normal sentences. However, if only one prisoner defects from the other, the defector will be released. Their silent partner will then serve a longer sentence.

In this version of the Prisoner's Dilemma, dubbed "Split or Steal", you must choose whether to split or steal points from a computer opponent over a series of 10-15 rounds. Your opponent will also make the same decision using one of the strategies listed below. Your goal is to beat your opponent, and try to gain as many points as possible.
